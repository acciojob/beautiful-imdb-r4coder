Baahubali 2: The Conclusion is a Telugu-language epic action-drama directed by S. S. Rajamouli. It is the sequel to Baahubali: The Beginning and concludes the story of Mahendra Baahubali (Shivudu) as he uncovers the truth about his father, Amarendra Baahubali, and seeks justice against the tyrannical king Bhallaladeva.
